Lasagne

Description:

Indulge in a the hearty and comforting flavours of a classic italian lasagne. This dish layers rich meat sauce, soft ricotta and perfectly cooked lasagne noodles, topped with a generous amount of melted cheese. Perfect for family dinners or special gatherings, this recipe is sure to become a beloved favourite.

Ingredients

Ingredients are customisable and you can adjust quantities to your liking.

  • Meat: Sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 cans of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • Seasoning: Parsley, dried basil, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, salt, and pepper
  • Lasagne noodles/sheets
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozarella, and ricotta
  • 1 egg

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagne (refer to steps below)
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Finish baking with foil off
  7. Let the lasagne rest before serving

How to layer Lasagne:

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozarella
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan
  7. Repeat the layers then top with the remaining Parmesan
