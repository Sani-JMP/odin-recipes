Adobo

Description:

Adobo is a beloved Filipino dish known for its rich, savory flavor and tender meat. Traditionally made with chicken or pork, it involves marinating the protein in a mixture of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and black peppercorns. The marinated meat is then simmered until tender, allowing the flavors to meld beautifully. Served over steamed rice, adobo is a comforting staple that reflects the heart of Filipino cuisine, with each family often adding their own unique twist. Whether enjoyed at a festive gathering or a simple home meal, adobo is a dish that embodies warmth and tradition.
